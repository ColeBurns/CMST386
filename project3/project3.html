Google Analytics Report Types

Google Analytics offers several reports that help track user behavior. First, the Audience Overview report provides demographic data, such as the user's location, age, and interests. This helps identify the type of audience visiting your site. Second, the Behavior Flow report shows how users navigate through the site, highlighting popular paths or drop-off points. This can help optimize content placement. Lastly, the Acquisition Report tracks how visitors found your site, whether through search engines, social media, or referrals. These reports help marketers and webmasters understand which channels and content resonate most with the target audience.
