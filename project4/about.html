Meet the Artist

P. David Cress

David started drawing in his youth, and has been a self-taught illustrator for over 20 years. Heavily inflenced by comics, manga, and video games, he continues to develop his own stylized and unique aesthetic while expanding upon his existing skill set by constantly using new tools and forms of media.



Most Commonly Used Media:


  • Papers: Fabriano Artistico 100% Cotton Acquarello Watercolor Paper (Hot Pressed, 140 lbs.), Arches 100% Cotton Watercolor Paper (Hot Pressed, 140 lbs.), & Strathmore 500-Series 100% Cotton Bristol (Plate)
  • Pencils: Staedtler Mars Tecnico 2mm Mechanical Pencil & Staedtler Night Blue 2mm Mechanical Pencil
  • Lead/Graphite: Prismacolor Turquoise Non-Photo Blue 2mm Lead, Koh-I-Noor 2mm Graphite (F), Mitsubishi 2mm Graphite (4H, 2H, HB & 2B)
  • Pens: Koh-I-Noor No 130 N Dip Pen & Tachikawa Model 40 Comic Pen Nib Holder
  • Inks: Kuretake ZIG Special Black Ink 60, Winsor Newton India Ink, & Higgins Caligraphy Black Ink
  • Colored Pencils: Prismacolor Premier Verithin Colored Pencils (Hard Core), Caran D'Ache Supracolor II Watercolor Pencils, & Pandafly Colour Charcoal Pencils
  • Markers: Ohuhu Alcohol Markers (Fine & Brush Tip), Winsor Newton Inks (Colored), Zeyar Acrylic Paint Pens, Chromatek Watercolor Paint Pens, Pilot Silver & Gold Paint Markers, & Artist's Loft Colored Fineliners
